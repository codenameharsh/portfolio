YonderLust: Explore Yourself

Case Study | Solo Travel App UI Design

Duration: 2 days

Overview

YonderLust is a mobile app designed to empower and inspire solo travelers to explore new places with confidence. The app curates local events, sightseeing spots, and activities tailored to independent adventurers, making solo trips safer, easier, and more exciting.

Problem

    Traveling alone can feel overwhelming:
  • Difficulty finding trusted local events and places
  • Lack of easy planning tools for solo activities
  • Safety concerns when exploring unfamiliar areas
  • Desire for a sense of community without needing a group

Solution

    YonderLust solves these challenges by offering:
  1. Personalized Recommendations: Local events and spots based on location and interests
  2. Visual Itinerary Planning: Easy-to-use planning features
  3. Safety Integrations: Quick SOS button and location sharing
  4. User-Friendly Design: Minimal, friendly UI focusing on exploration

Target Audience

  • Solo travelers (ages 18–40)
  • Students, digital nomads, explorers
  • Travelers who seek independence but also safety and guidance

Design Process

  1. Wireframing:
    • Started with grayscale wireframes to focus purely on layout and usability.
    • Ensured easy navigation: Home, Explore, Events.
    • Map integration at the homepage for immediate spatial awareness.
  2. Branding:
    • Logo: A walking shoe sketch representing adventure and personal journeys.
    • Tagline: "Explore Yourself." — Encouraging self-discovery through travel
    • Color Palette: Soft teal and charcoal for a balance of calm, trust, and excitement.
  3. High-Fidelity Prototypes:
    • Bold, inviting imagery for events and places.
    • Clear, rounded buttons and cards for easy tapping.
    • Map pins for nearby events and points of interest.
    • Safety and itinerary features placed prominently.

Key Features

  1. Personalized Home Dashboard
  2. Local Events and Places to Explore
  3. Plan Your Itinerary Easily
  4. Quick SOS Access and Safety Features
  5. Clean, Friendly UI for Stress-Free Navigation

Tools Used

  • Figma: UI design + prototyping
  • Illustrator: For icons and vector elements
  • Photoshop: Image prep
  • Google Maps API: Mock integration for map features

Reflection

Designing YonderLust taught me the importance of balancing excitement and safety for solo travelers. I focused heavily on making the app feel empowering, intuitive, and welcoming. If I expand this project, I would love to add social features like "meet other solo travelers" and smart recommendations based on real-time location data.
